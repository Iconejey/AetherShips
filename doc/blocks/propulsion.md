# Propulsion (110-139)

Propulsion blocks provide thrust and movement capabilities to ships and stations. They must be placed asymmetrically to generate rotational torque in addition to linear movement.

---

## Electric Thruster
**Craft:** Iron Ingot + Copper Wire

The entry-level thruster, powered purely by stored electrical energy. Low thrust output but cheap to build and operate. Best suited for small, lightweight ships.

---

## Bio-Fuel Thruster
**Craft:** Steel + Electronic Component

A mid-tier thruster that burns Bio-Fuel for propulsion. Offers considerably more thrust than the electric thruster, with a renewable fuel source from Greenhouses and Bio-Refineries.

---

## Uranium Thruster
**Craft:** Titanium Ingot + Processor

A high-performance thruster fueled by Refined Uranium. Delivers exceptional thrust and acceleration, at the cost of requiring a closely monitored fuel source.

---

## Warp Drive
**Craft:** Titanium Ingot + Processor + Cut Crystal

A rare and expensive device that allows near-instantaneous travel across sectors. Crystal optics and a precision processor are needed to safely bend space. Only the most advanced ships can afford one.
