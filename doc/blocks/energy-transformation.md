# Energy & Transformation (80-109)

Energy and transformation blocks generate, store, and convert power for ship systems, and process raw resources into usable materials.

---

## Solar Panel - Tier 1
**Craft:** Iron Ingot + Copper Wire + Glass

The simplest photovoltaic panel. Converts sunlight into electrical energy. Efficiency depends on proximity to a star. Ideal for small, budget-friendly ships.

---

## Solar Panel - Tier 2
**Craft:** Electronic Component + Copper Wire + Glass

An improved solar panel with better photovoltaic cells. Generates more power from the same amount of sunlight compared to Tier 1.

---

## Solar Panel - Tier 3
**Craft:** Processor + Copper Wire + Cut Crystal

A high-efficiency panel using crystal optics to focus and convert solar energy. Suitable for mid-to-late-game ships requiring substantial power.

---

## Solar Panel - Tier 4
**Craft:** Processor + Cut Crystal + Titanium Ingot

The most advanced solar panel available. Titanium framing and crystal lenses maximize energy output even at significant distances from a star.

---

## Basic Capacitor
**Craft:** Iron Ingot + Copper Wire

A simple energy storage unit. Stores electrical energy generated by solar panels or other sources for use when demand spikes.

---

## High-Density Capacitor
**Craft:** Electronic Component + Titanium Ingot

An advanced capacitor with far greater storage capacity. Essential for ships with high-power weapons or drives that require burst energy.

---

## Industrial Refinery
**Craft:** Iron Hull - Tier 1 + Copper Wire

The workhorse of material processing. Smelts ores into ingots and refines raw materials into usable components. Required for the vast majority of crafting recipes.

---

## Uranium Refinery
**Craft:** Lead plate + Electronic Component

A heavily shielded refinery designed to safely process Uranium Ore into Refined Uranium. Lead plating is essential to contain radiation. Possession is watched closely by the U.R.A.

---

## Bio-Refinery
**Craft:** Iron Hull - Tier 1 + Copper Wire

A biological processing unit that converts Vegetation into Bio-Fuel. Provides a sustainable and off-grid energy supply, independent of U.R.A. resources.

---

## Greenhouse
**Craft:** Glass Window + Dirt

A transparent enclosure filled with soil that cultivates Vegetation over time. Enables a fully renewable Bio-Fuel supply chain on ships or stations.
