# Refined Materials & Components (20-49)

These blocks are produced by processing raw resources through refineries or by assembling simpler components. They serve as intermediate materials for building more advanced structures and equipment.

---

## Iron Ingot
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Iron Ore

Pure smelted iron. The most basic structural and mechanical material in the game, used in a wide range of components and hulls.

---

## Copper Ingot
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Copper Ore

Refined copper metal. Primarily processed further into Copper Wire for electrical applications.

---

## Titanium Ingot
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Titanium Ore

A lightweight yet incredibly strong metal. Used in high-tier hulls, advanced weapons, and top-end ship systems.

---

## Carbon
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Coal

Purified carbon extracted from coal. Combined with Iron Ingots to produce Steel.

---

## Steel
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Iron Ingot + Carbon

A strong alloy of iron and carbon. Offers significantly better structural integrity than plain iron, used in Tier 2 hulls and advanced components.

---

## Glass
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Sand

Transparent material produced by melting sand. Used in windows, solar panels, and optical components.

---

## Cut Crystal
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Raw Crystal

Precisely shaped and polished crystal. Used in high-end processors, solar panels, and energy weapons.

---

## Refined Uranium
**Craft:** Uranium Refinery + Uranium Ore

Processed fissile material that serves as a high-density power source. Its production is tightly controlled by the U.R.A.

---

## Bio-Fuel
**Craft:** Bio-Refinery + Vegetation

Organic fuel refined from plant matter. A sustainable and renewable energy source for thrusters and reactors.

---

## Copper Wire
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Copper Ingot

Thin conductive strands drawn from copper ingots. An essential ingredient in virtually every electronic component.

---

## Electronic Component
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Copper Wire + Iron Ingot

Basic circuits and assemblies built from wire and metal. The fundamental building block of all electronics.

---

## Processor
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Electronic Component + Cut Crystal

A sophisticated computing unit combining crystal optics with electronic circuits. Required for high-tier ship systems and automation.

---

## Lead plate
**Craft:** Industrial Refinery + Lead Ore

Dense flat sheets of lead. Used to line containers and components that handle radioactive or volatile materials, providing critical shielding.
