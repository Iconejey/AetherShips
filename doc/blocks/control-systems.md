# Control Systems (Cores) (200-229)

Control system blocks are the brains of a ship or station. Ship Cores define a vessel's capabilities and tier, while Actuators execute automated navigation and combat instructions.

---

## Ship Core - Tier 1
**Craft:** Iron Hull - Tier 1 + Electronic Component

The most basic ship core. Required to register a vessel and enable navigation mode. Supports simple automations and low-tier systems only.

---

## Ship Core - Tier 2
**Craft:** Steel Hull - Tier 2 + Processor

A more capable core that unlocks mid-tier systems and more complex automation scripts. The backbone of a reliable mid-game vessel.

---

## Ship Core - Tier 3
**Craft:** Titanium Hull - Tier 3 + Processor

An advanced core providing increased processing power for navigation, automation, and system management on high-tier ships.

---

## Ship Core - Tier 4
**Craft:** Alloy Hull - Tier 4 + Processor + Cut Crystal

The pinnacle of ship control technology. Supports the full range of systems and the most sophisticated automation configurations.

---

## Actuator - Tier 1
**Craft:** Iron Ingot + Copper Wire

A basic mechanical actuator. Executes simple triggered actions such as firing thrusters or toggling systems. Essential for entry-level automation.

---

## Actuator - Tier 2
**Craft:** Steel + Electronic Component

An improved actuator with better response speed and support for more complex action sequences.

---

## Actuator - Tier 3
**Craft:** Titanium Ingot + Processor

A high-precision actuator capable of handling demanding automation chains with minimal latency.

---

## Actuator - Tier 4
**Craft:** Titanium Ingot + Processor + Cut Crystal

The most advanced actuator, with crystal-enhanced precision. Supports the full breadth of navigation and combat automation available in the game.

---

## Cryo Chamber
**Craft:** Electronic Component + Glass

A life-support pod installed aboard a ship or station. Upon the player's death, the Cryo Chamber preserves their neural pattern and respawns them at its location. Without one, respawning is impossible in the field.
