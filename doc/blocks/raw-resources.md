# Raw Resources (0-19)

Raw resources are found in the environment — on planets, asteroids, or in the ground. They cannot be crafted and must be mined directly.

---

## Rock
**Cannot be crafted** — mined from planets and asteroids.

The most common solid material in the universe. It has no direct use but can be broken down by refineries.

---

## Iron Ore
**Cannot be crafted** — mined from planets and asteroids.

A common metallic ore found across most star systems. Smelted into Iron Ingots for structural and mechanical use.

---

## Copper Ore
**Cannot be crafted** — mined from planets and asteroids.

A reddish conductive ore used to produce Copper Ingots and, by extension, Copper Wire and electronics.

---

## Titanium Ore
**Cannot be crafted** — mined from planets and asteroids, more common near the galaxy's outer regions.

A rare, ultra-strong ore refined into Titanium Ingots for high-tier components and hulls.

---

## Lead Ore
**Cannot be crafted** — mined from planets and asteroids.

A dense, heavy ore refined into lead plates. Useful for radiation shielding in uranium-handling components.

---

## Uranium Ore
**Cannot be crafted** — mined from restricted U.R.A.-controlled deposits.

An extremely rare and radioactive ore. It can only be processed by a Uranium Refinery, and its possession is monitored by the U.R.A.

---

## Coal
**Cannot be crafted** — mined from planets.

A carbon-rich mineral burned for fuel or refined into Carbon for advanced alloys.

---

## Sand
**Cannot be crafted** — found on desert planets.

Fine granular material used to produce Glass when processed through a refinery.

---

## Raw Crystal
**Cannot be crafted** — found in crystal-rich asteroid fields or deep planetary veins.

A translucent mineral that becomes Cut Crystal after refinement. Used in advanced optics, processors, and energy systems.

---

## Dirt
**Cannot be crafted** — found on terrestrial planets.

Loose organic-mineral soil. Primarily used in Greenhouses to grow vegetation.

---

## Vegetation
**Cannot be crafted** — harvested from planets or grown in Greenhouses.

Organic plant matter that can be refined into Bio-Fuel, providing a renewable energy source.
