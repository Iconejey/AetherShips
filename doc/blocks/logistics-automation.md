# Logistics & Automation (170-199)

Logistics blocks handle the storage and transport of resources and other materials aboard ships and stations.

---

## Industrial Container
**Craft:** Iron Hull - Tier 1

A general-purpose storage unit for solid materials and components. The standard container for carrying raw ores, ingots, and manufactured goods.

---

## Bio-Fuel Container
**Craft:** Iron Hull - Tier 1 + Lead plate

A sealed container for storing Bio-Fuel. Lead-lined walls prevent leakage and contamination of other cargo.

---

## Uranium Container
**Craft:** Lead plate + Titanium Ingot

A heavily shielded container built to safely store Refined Uranium. Its thick lead and titanium construction prevents radiation exposure to crew and equipment.

---

## Rack
**Craft:** Iron Ingot

A docking rail that allows ships, drones, and other entities to dock, travel along the rack, and take off. Enables modular ship designs and automated transport of sub-entities.
