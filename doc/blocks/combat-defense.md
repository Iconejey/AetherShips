# Combat & Defense (140-169)

Combat and defense blocks allow ships to attack enemies, mine resources, and protect themselves from incoming fire.

---

## Cannon - Tier 1
**Craft:** Iron Ingot + Copper Wire

A basic kinetic cannon that fires iron slugs. Low damage and range, but inexpensive and easy to mount on early-game ships.

---

## Cannon - Tier 2
**Craft:** Steel + Electronic Component

An improved cannon with better barrel materials and electronic targeting assistance. Deals more damage and fires more reliably than Tier 1.

---

## Cannon - Tier 3
**Craft:** Titanium Ingot + Processor

A high-velocity cannon with a processor-assisted targeting system. Capable of penetrating reinforced hulls.

---

## Cannon - Tier 4
**Craft:** Titanium Ingot + Processor + Cut Crystal

The most powerful cannon available. Crystal-enhanced optics combined with a titanium barrel deliver devastating precision fire.

---

## Explosive Charge - Tier 1
**Craft:** Iron Ingot + Coal

A crude improvised explosive. Useful for demolishing low-tier structures or rock formations.

---

## Explosive Charge - Tier 2
**Craft:** Steel + Carbon

A refined explosive with greater destructive yield. Suitable for mid-tier combat or mining in tougher terrain.

---

## Explosive Charge - Tier 3
**Craft:** Titanium Ingot + Refined Uranium

A radioactive high-yield warhead. Capable of destroying reinforced hulls. Requires careful handling due to its uranium core.

---

## Shield Generator
**Craft:** Titanium Ingot + Processor + Cut Crystal

Generates an energy barrier around the ship that absorbs incoming damage. Requires a significant power draw and must be paired with Shield Plasma to function.

---

## Shield plasma
**Craft:** Refined Uranium + Cut Crystal

The active medium within a shield, produced from uranium-crystal interactions. Fills the barrier generated by a Shield Generator to absorb and dissipate incoming projectiles and energy.
