# Background

Since the game takes place in space, there's a risk of having a repetitive and simple background. To provide visual interest and cues for the player, it's essential to avoid a black and empty background.

## Background Stars

Unlike the sector stars that the player might encounter, background stars are simply small points of light that populate the game screen's background. They are completely random and are not saved.

They move in the opposite direction to the player to give the sensation of movement and speed. The brightest stars move faster, creating a parallax effect that adds depth to the scene.

## Nebulae

Nebulae are blurry, colored shapes that are generated and saved when the galaxy is created. Their position will always remain the same.

Their purpose is to add color, but also to allow the player to orient themselves with fixed visual landmarks.

Their coords are 8 times smaller than the galaxy's, so they move 8 times slower than the player, creating a strong parallax effect that enhances the sensation of depth and vastness in space.
