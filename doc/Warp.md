# Warp

To travel long distances, the player can use warp drives and warp gates. They consume a lot of energy but allow the ship to jump to another location in space instantly, bypassing any obstacles in between.

## Warp Drive

Warp drives allow the ship that the user is currently controlling to jump to another location in space. They consume energy based on the distance traveled, and have a maximum range that can be increased by adding more warp drive blocks.

## Warp Gate

When an entity, likely a station, has a warp gate, it can be used by the player to jump to another entity with a warp gate if in range. This allows the player to travel across the galaxy by jumping from one station to another, without needing to have a warp drive on their ship or worrying about energy (only the entity with the warp gate consumes energy, not the ship).
